Final Project

My program is meant to be a replica of the mobile game Geometry Dash. The game is a sidescroller game. The objective of the game is to avoid the spikes as you move towards them by jumping. You can avoid the spikes by pressing and holdind the space button to avoid the spikes. Once you make it past all of the spikes you win the game.
